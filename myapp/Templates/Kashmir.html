Places to see in Kashmir

1. Srinagar:Srinagar, the capital of Kashmir valley is famously known for its world renowned Dal lake,Nigeen lake,Mughal gardens, Tulip garden, Dachigam national park among manyother heritage and scenic places. Accommodation of all star categories including world famous brands like Sheraton, Taj group, Radisson among other local heritage brand like Ahad group of hotels are available in srinagar. Houseboat stay at dal lake is common feature of all the "Tour packages of kashmir".

2.Gulmarg: Asia's best ski resort is a promise of scenery, beauty, landscapes, mountains, forest, Gondola, Skiing. One should stop by at Tangmarg before finally moving to gulmarg to feed your cameras with unmatched beauty. Zig point also offers uninterrupted view of north kashmir. Another famous place in the vicinity of Gulmarg is Baba Rishi and Shrunz fall. Kongdori, affarwat, Strawberry valley are most visited points in "Gulmarg tour". "kashmir Holiday" is incomplete without a night stay in Gulmarg.

3.Pahalgam: Pahalgam is a hill station in Jammu and Kashmir. Aru Wildlife is home to animals including brown bears and musk deer. Northeast, the Lidder River runs through scenic Betaab Valley. Southeast, Tulian Lake is flanked by mountain peaks and often frozen. Premium 4 star and 3 star hotels are available for all Tourists in Pahalgam.

4.Sonamarg:A hill station in the Gaderbal district of Jammu and kashmir often called as gateway to ladakh is a meadow of Gold. The road that leads to sonamarg runs parallel to River Sindh with snow clad mountains in the backdrop one literally feels close to heaven. Thajwas glacier and zojila pass are the two major sightseeing attraction for the tourists.

Best season to visit kashmir

Spring season( March 15th to May 15th) :Best time to visit Kashmir if you love flowers. Tulip garden, Badamwari,snow clad mountains, Cool breeze and charming weather are specialties of spring season.

Summer season ( May 15th to August 31st) :Best time to visit Kashmir if you love camping by river side, warm temperatures and trekking to glaciers.

Autumn season:( 01st September to November 15th ) :The season of fall, also know as harvest season, one can see Red apples in apple garden, enjoy the local organic produce like Walnut, almonds and much more.

Winter season:( 15th November to 15th march ) :Skiing in Gulmarg, Heli skiing in Gulmarg, Romantic trips, Cold weather, snow, snowflakes etc. are some common features of winter in Kashmir.
